About Me

AOM

My Journey

From a coffee town in Risaralda to building across continents

~1998

Bulteador & Café Waiter — Belén de Umbría

First jobs as a teenager in a small coffee town in Risaralda, Colombia. Unloaded heavy sacks of coffee and plantain from jeeps at a café agency during weekends, and waited tables at a local café — all while studying secondary school.

~2000

Folk Dance Teacher

Turned a passion for traditional Colombian folk dance into teaching. Led classes and performed, building early skills in leadership, discipline, and cultural expression.

2001

Colombian National Police — Patrullero

Joined the Colombian National Police. Completed one year of training as Patrullero, followed by two years of service as a professional police officer — learning discipline, structure, and public service.

2004

Moved to London — Cleaner at Westminster

Emigrated to London from Colombia. Started working as a cleaner at the University of Westminster — the same building where, years later, he would graduate with First Class Honours.

2006

Westminster University — BSc Mobile Communications

Enrolled in BSc Mobile Communications Engineering at the University of Westminster — trading the mop for a lecture hall in the same building. Graduated four years later with First Class Honours.

2008

Freelance Web Developer

Started freelancing, building websites and web applications for small businesses in London.

2009

Telecom Engineer — WiMAX

Worked in telecommunications, earning WiMAX certification and gaining deep expertise in network infrastructure.

2011

MBA — University of Wales

Completed Master of Business Administration at the University of Wales Trinity Saint David, blending tech with business strategy.

2013

Return to Colombia — Teaching & Entrepreneurship

Returned to Colombia to teach university courses and launch entrepreneurial ventures in education and technology.

2015

Published First Books

Began publishing career with books on business, cryptocurrency, and emotional intelligence. Now 11+ titles on Amazon.

2017

Ciudad Bilingue Platform

Built a bilingual education platform connecting language learners with tutors, scaling to hundreds of users.

2018

IP Diploma — UPB

Earned Intellectual Property Diploma from Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana, adding legal expertise to the toolkit.

2020

Axiom & TutorBox Launch

Launched Axiom project management platform and TutorBox tutoring marketplace. Full-stack development with modern frameworks.

2022

GeoRef & La Lupa

Built GeoRef (geographic reference platform) and La Lupa (investigative data tool). Expanding into GIS and data analytics.

2024

Google Analytics Certified

Added Google Analytics certification, strengthening data-driven decision making and digital strategy capabilities.
